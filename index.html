Mrijula Thinks

Short Documentary

In a quiet corner of Majuli lives Mrijula — a woman whose way of seeing the world feels simple, yet deeply profound. Her thoughts on fear, love, and the self aren’t academic; they’re lived, honest, and powerful enough to shift something in you.

As I listened to her, something in me began to change. This film follows Mrijula’s philosophy — and my own quiet journey of facing fear and seeing life differently through her eyes. Watch the documentary
